Local Shops & Markets

Discover Local Commerce

Long Bedian's local shops and markets offer a unique shopping experience where you can find authentic Kayan crafts, fresh jungle produce, traditional foods, and everyday essentials. These community-run businesses are the heart of local commerce, supporting both residents and visitors with quality goods and warm hospitality.

Traditional Crafts & Handicrafts

What You Can Find

  • Beadwork: Intricate traditional jewelry, accessories, and decorative items
  • Rattan Weaving: Baskets, mats, and containers made from natural rattan
  • Wood Carvings: Traditional decorative items and functional pieces
  • Traditional Textiles: Hand-woven fabrics and traditional clothing

Shopping Tips for Visitors

Best Times to Visit

Early mornings are ideal for fresh produce markets, while craft shops are typically open throughout the day. Weekend markets often have more vendors and a wider selection of items.

Payment & Bargaining

Most shops accept cash (Malaysian Ringgit). While prices are generally fixed, friendly negotiation is sometimes acceptable, especially for handmade crafts. Always be respectful and fair in your dealings.
