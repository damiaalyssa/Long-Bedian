Natural Attractions and Adventure

Rumah Panjang Long Bedian

Step into the heart of Long Bedian heritage at the Long Bedian Longhouse. Built in the 1950s, it offers an authentic glimpse of traditional Sarawakian life. Made from durable Bornean hardwood, itâ€™s both a living home and a cultural landmark.

Local Crafts Market

Visit the local market area to purchase authentic, handmade Kayan crafts and fresh jungle produce.

Taman Rekreasi Sungai Mirah Tenyok

A peaceful nature spot featuring clear river views, lush greenery, and relaxing picnic areas. Perfect for family outings, swimming, and enjoying the calm atmosphere of the rainforest.

Tenyuk Waterfall

An hour from Kampung Long Bedian, offer a serene escape into untouched Bornean nature. Safe for swimming, visitors can enjoy camping, picnics, and river activities like kayaking and boating.
