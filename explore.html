Places to Go, Things to Do

Natural Attractions and Adventure

Jungle Waterfalls

Guided treks lead to pristine waterfalls hidden deep within the primary rainforest, offering a refreshing natural pool for a swim.

Baram River Caves

Explore smaller limestone caves accessible by longboat, historically used by tribespeople for shelter or burial rites.

Guided Nature Walks

Learn about traditional medicinal plants, hunting techniques, and local fauna from experienced community guides.

Kayan Craft Workshops

Participate in hands-on workshops to learn beadwork, Sape' playing basics, or rattan weaving from local artisans.

Local Crafts Market

Visit the local market area to purchase authentic, handmade Kayan crafts and fresh jungle produce.

Homestays and Guest Houses

Experience Authentic Living

To truly experience Kayan life, we encourage visitors to stay in a **local Homestay**. These guest houses, often part of the longhouse structure or adjacent homes, offer clean, comfortable accommodation and delicious traditional meals.

  • Shared Longhouse Rooms: Experience communal living directly.
  • Private Guesthouses: For visitors preferring more privacy.
  • Traditional Meals: Included with most stays.

Booking inquiries for homestays are handled via the Contact Us page.
